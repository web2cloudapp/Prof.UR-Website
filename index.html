Biography

I am currently a Professor in the Department of Computer and System Sciences in Visva-Bharati University, Santiniketan, West Bengal, India. I joined the department on October 23, 1997.

My research interests include Computer Vision, Machine Learning, Pattern Recognition, and Data Analytics.

I have supervised numerous PhD scholars who have received their Degree, with several more currently pursuing their PhD work under my guidance.

I am a member of various professional organizations and have received recognition for my contributions to the field of Computer Science and Information Technology.
